This is a course on how ecological systems change through time and how to forecast how they will change in the future. It combines reading and discussing primary scientific literature with R tutorials on how to work with time-series data and make forecasts in R.

{{< /blocks/section >}} {{< blocks/section >}} {{% blocks/feature icon="fa-lightbulb" title="Taught @UF" %}} This course is taught each Fall at the University of Florida by Drs. Morgan Ernest and Ethan White. {{% /blocks/feature %}} {{% blocks/feature icon="fa-book" title="Learn on Your Own" %}} The full course including lecture notes and R tutorials is openly available on this site so that anyone can learn these important approaches and skills for themselves. {{% /blocks/feature %}} {{% blocks/feature icon="fab fa-github" title="Use & Contribute" url="https://github.com/weecology/forecasting-course" %}} Course materials are openly licensed and developed on GitHub.
